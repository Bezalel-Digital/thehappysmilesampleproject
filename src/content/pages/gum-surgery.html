Healthier Gums, Stronger Smile – Expert Care You Can Trust.

At Happy Smile Dental Clinic, we understand that healthy gums are essential for a strong, beautiful smile. Dr. Yseult, our cosmetic dentistry head and our team specialize in advanced gum surgery procedures to treat gum disease, gum recession, and other periodontal issues.

Our Gum Surgery Process


At Happy Smile Dental Clinic, we provide a comprehensive, patient-focused approach to gum surgery:

1. Thorough Gum Assessment


Your treatment begins with a detailed evaluation of your gums, where we:

  • Check for signs of gum disease, recession, or infection.
  • Use digital imaging to assess bone and gum health.
  • Develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs.

2. Gentle & Precise Gum Treatment


Depending on your gum condition, we may recommend:

  • Scaling and Root Planing – A deep cleaning to remove bacteria and smooth the roots for better healing.
  • Gum Grafting – If your gums have receded, we can restore lost tissue using a gum graft.
  • Flap Surgery – If deep pockets are present, we gently lift the gums, clean the area, and secure them back for proper healing.
  • Laser Gum Surgery – A minimally invasive option that reduces discomfort and promotes quicker recovery.

3. Comfortable & Stress-Free Procedure


We prioritize patient comfort by using:

  • Local anesthesia to numb the area.
  • Sedation options for a pain-free experience.
  • Advanced techniques to reduce downtime and promote healing.

4. Post-Surgery Care & Follow-Up


After your gum surgery, we provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure a smooth recovery. Our team will also schedule follow-ups to monitor your healing progress.
