Our General Dentistry Services


At Happy Smile Dental Clinic, we offer a wide range of general dentistry treatments tailored to your needs:

1. Routine Dental Check-Ups & Exams


Regular dental check-ups are essential for early detection of oral health issues. During your visit, we will:

  • Examine your teeth and gums for signs of decay, cavities, or infection.
  • Perform oral cancer screenings.
  • Take digital X-rays (if needed) for a detailed assessment.

2. Professional Teeth Cleaning (Scale & Polish)


A professional cleaning removes plaque, tartar, and surface stains, leaving your teeth healthy and refreshed. Regular cleanings help prevent gum disease and maintain long-lasting oral health.

3. Tooth-Colored Fillings & Cavity Treatment


If you have a cavity or minor tooth damage, our tooth-colored composite fillings restore your tooth’s strength and appearance, blending seamlessly with your natural teeth.

4. Gum Disease Treatment


Healthy gums are the foundation of a strong smile. We provide scaling and root planing (deep cleaning) to treat early gum disease and prevent further complications.

5. Tooth Extractions (Including Wisdom Teeth Removal)


If a tooth is damaged, decayed, or impacted, we offer gentle and pain-free tooth extractions, including wisdom tooth removal, to prevent further oral health issues.

6. Fluoride Treatments & Dental Sealants


  • Fluoride treatments strengthen enamel and prevent cavities.
  • Dental sealants protect back teeth (molars) from decay, making them great for children and adults alike.

7. Custom Mouthguards & Nightguards


We offer custom-made mouthguards for sports protection and nightguards to prevent teeth grinding (bruxism), reducing the risk of enamel wear and jaw pain.
