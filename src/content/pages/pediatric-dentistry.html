Protect, Prevent, and Preserve – Your Path to Lifelong Oral Health!

At Happy Smile Dental Clinic, we understand that a child’s early dental experiences shape their lifelong oral health. Our pediatric dentistry services are designed to make dental visits fun, comfortable, and stress-free. Led by Dr. Raj Fashek, our experienced pediatric team provides gentle, preventive, and specialized dental care to help children maintain strong, cavity-free smiles.

Our Pediatric Dental Services


At Happy Smile Dental Clinic, we provide a child-friendly, welcoming environment where kids feel safe and comfortable. Our pediatric services include:

1. First Dental Visit & Check-Ups


We recommend scheduling your child’s first dental visit by age one or when their first tooth appears. Our gentle check-ups include:

  • A complete oral exam to assess tooth and gum health.
  • Parental guidance on proper brushing, flossing, and nutrition.
  • Fun, interactive education to make dental care exciting for kids.

2. Gentle Cleanings & Preventive Care


  • A complete oral exam to assess tooth and gum health.Professional cleanings to remove plaque buildup and prevent cavities.
  • Fluoride treatments to strengthen enamel and protect against decay.
  • Dental sealants for added protection on molars, reducing cavity risk.

3. Cavity Protection & Tooth-Colored Fillings


Even with great hygiene, kids can get cavities. We provide:

  • Minimally invasive, tooth-colored fillings that blend naturally.
  • Painless treatments using advanced techniques to keep your child comfortable.

4. Early Orthodontic Assessments


We monitor jaw development and tooth alignment to detect potential orthodontic issues early. Early intervention can prevent more extensive treatments later on.

5. Gentle Extractions & Space Maintainers


If a baby tooth needs to be removed early due to decay or crowding, we ensure a pain-free extraction and may recommend a space maintainer to keep teeth properly aligned.

6. Emergency Pediatric Dental Care


Kids are active, and dental emergencies happen! Whether it’s a knocked-out tooth, dental pain, or injury, our team provides immediate and compassionate care to ease discomfort and protect your child’s smile.
