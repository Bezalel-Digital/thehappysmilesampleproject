Our Dental Implant Process


At Happy Smile Dental Clinic, we follow a patient-centered, step-by-step approach to ensure a smooth implant experience:

1. Consultation & Evaluation


Your journey begins with a comprehensive consultation. Our team will:

  • Examine your oral health.
  • Take digital X-rays and 3D scans.
  • Assess your bone density and gum condition.

If you’re a good candidate, we’ll create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs.

2. Implant Placement


The next step is implant surgery, where we precisely place the titanium implant into your jawbone. This procedure is performed under local anesthesia or sedation to ensure comfort.

3. Healing & Osseointegration


Over the next few months, the implant will naturally bond with your jawbone through a process called osseointegration, creating a strong foundation for your new tooth.

4. Abutment & Crown Placement


Once healed, we attach a small connector (abutment) and a custom-made crown, carefully designed to match your natural teeth.

5. Aftercare & Follow-Up


We provide detailed aftercare instructions and schedule follow-ups to monitor your implant’s success.
